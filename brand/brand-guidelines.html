01 — Identity

Logo

The wordmark is QUEST in ink with a ✦ in VNG orange, set in Space Grotesk Bold with tight tracking (−0.02em). The ✦ is the one constant — it carries the brand.

QUEST✦
QUEST✦

On light, QUEST is ink (#16212e). On dark, QUEST turns white — the ✦ always stays orange.

Clear space & size

Keep clear space around the wordmark equal to the height of the ✦. Minimum legible size ≈ 18px tall on screen. Use logo-mark.svg (the ✦ alone) as a favicon, bullet, or app-icon seed.

Do & don't

✓ Do

  • Keep QUEST ink (or white on dark) and the ✦ orange.
  • Use Space Grotesk Bold, tracking −0.02em.
  • Give it room; let it sit on a calm background.

✕ Don't

  • Recolor QUEST orange or drop the ✦.
  • Swap the font, stretch, outline, or add shadows/glow.
  • Place it on a busy image without a backing.

Rules for every slide

✓ Do

  • Light background with the radial wash; dark ink surface for section dividers.
  • One idea per slide; generous margins.
  • At most one gradient accent per slide (a phrase or a number).
  • SVG icons from one set (e.g. Lucide); tabular numbers for data.
  • QUEST✦ small in a corner; ✦ as the bullet.

✕ Don't

  • Decorative chips/pills/sub-labels that just restate the title.
  • Multiple gradients or full-heading gradients competing for attention.
  • Emoji as functional icons; mixed icon styles.
  • Off-palette colors or a second display font.
  • Walls of text — split into more slides instead.
07 — Voice & naming

Words

Vietnamese-first; keep common English tech words (app, quiz, link, bot) rather than forcing full translation. Game-themed but plain. The cast names below appear across product and pitch.

Người dẫn đườngTeacher / class owner — creates the class, uploads materials, tracks everyone.
Anh hùngLearner — runs their own personalized quests.
ĐộiClass / group learning from the same material.
Nhiệm vụQuest — a ~5-minute pack of questions built from a document.
Thử tháchChallenge — a single question inside a quest.
Bảng theo dõiDashboard — the class-progress view for the teacher.
Tone: honest encouragement — never baiting ("mồi"), never blame the audience ("khó đến với từng người" ✓, not "không ai học" ✗). Quest is a team project, not a VNG Games product — no business-unit attribution.
